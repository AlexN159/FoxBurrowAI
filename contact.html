Tell FoxBurrowAI about your data and what you want to see.

Regardless of data format, FoxBurrowAI can help turn it into clean, sensible categories and clear dashboards. The simplest way to start is to share a small sample and tell us what decisions you’re trying to support.

What FoxBurrowAI can do for you

  • Categorise large invoice / PO datasets into multi-level taxonomies (e.g. UNSPSC-style L1–L4) from around £0.075 per line (≈$0.10).
  • Cope with a wide range of Excel formats – multiple currencies, varying column names, and different date/amount layouts are no problem.
  • Provide ranked category options and short explanations per line, so reviewers can quickly accept or adjust.
  • Deliver tailored dashboards as simple, self-contained tools with no user logins required.

What happens next?

  1. We’ll reply to understand your objectives, timelines, and any constraints (e.g. data staying on-premise).
  2. If appropriate, we’ll ask for a small sample Excel file or anonymised extract so we can run a short trial.
  3. You’ll receive a sample categorised file and (optionally) a small dashboard, along with an outline of pricing.
  4. After confirmation and acceptence of categorisation, we scale up to the full dataset and agree on ongoing refreshes or one-off deliverables.
